Our Mission

The 1st.KY Cavalry Det. was created to provide a special service to all members of the armed services, Active, Veterans, as well to Civic and Public Citizens Free. Also to promote Honor and Patriotism in all activities requested to attend such as but not limited too. Any Veteran activity, Honor Guard, Funerals, Parades. Etc.
Our Aim is to have at least 1 troop in every state to provide the service to our veterans and others who request it. Our Troops at this time are being set up and activated. 1. If you would like to join

If you would like to be apart of this organization we would love to hear from you. We are looking for experienced riders, However we are also looking for riders and non riders. for unexperienced riders you are welcome to train and become one of us. Note. We do not do mounted drills at this time... Our activities are Mounted Flag barriers, Mounted Escort, Ceremonial Honors. Each Troop will have a 6 or more man squad consisting of 4 mounted Troopers, 2 flag barriers and one out rider on outside of flagmen. Here is a list of all positions needed: 1. Mounted Trooper flag barrier & non flag Barrier. 2. support staff. a. horse handler b. driver/ horse handler c. quartermaster. d. Bugler. 3. National Staff positions available . a. Natl. Vice CDR. b. Natl. Coordinator. c. Web Pager MGR 4. State Staff positions available (contact person, may also have other duties such as rider etc.)
