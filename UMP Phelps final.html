Our Mission

The USWP Volunteer Mounted Patrol was created in 2010 to aid the US Water Patrol with Homeland Security Support and Public Safety operations on land.

The USWP Mounted Patrol Team is a all volunteer organization and is used for a variety of community functions including

  • Homeland Security Support Patrols
  • Search of lost children and adults
  • Search & Rescue.
  • Assisting the water patrol in SAR & HLS/PS patrols
  • Public, Safety patrols
  • Security patrols for private citizens & business
  • Assist in lost and/or missing horse, cattle. search and round-up
  • Fence line and property line patrol.




U.S. Water Patrol Sponsors

The USWPMP also is a partner with the American Red Cross. We provide horses and volunteers to assist in Disaster Response anywhere in the U.S. and/or it's territories.
